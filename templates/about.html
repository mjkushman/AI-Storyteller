About AI Write Along

What is AI Write Along?

Write Along is a tool to let people write short with an AI co-writing assistant. Since the AI writing assistant is unpredictable, you as an author won't really know where the story is going. It's like literary jazz!

second paragraph

How does it work?

A story starts with a genre and a starter prompt.

  1. Choose a genre and a starter prompt. You can write your own prompt or use a random one.
  2. Write a sentence or two to continue the story.
  3. The AI assistant will take a turn adding to the story.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 as desired

Behind the scenes:

The AI assistant powered by OpenAI (the makers of ChatGPT) is asked to co-write a story according to the genre and prompt.

  • Each time a user makes an addition to the story, an API call is made to OpenAI.
  • The API call contains most or all of the current story, including who wrote which parts.
  • The AI assistant sends back a short extension story, which gets appended to the overall story.
